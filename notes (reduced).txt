Complete Blockchain Entry System for the Kashmiri Handicrafts Sector
This system ensures full transparency from raw material sourcing to buyer transactions, tracking every step in the handicraft production and sales process.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Raw Material Sourcing (Waguvar – Wool, Silk, Walnut Wood, Papier-Mâché Base, etc.)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Ensures transparency in sourcing materials from farmers and suppliers.

Entry Type               | Data Fields
------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------
Raw Material Harvesting | Supplier ID, Material Type, Harvest Date, Quantity, 
                         | Source Location, Certification (e.g., organic)
                         | Blockchain: Private

Supplier Verification    | Supplier Name, Govt. ID, License Number, Past Transactions
                         | Blockchain: Private

Quality Certification    | Lab Test Report, Certification ID (Silk Mark, GI Tag),
                         | Certifying Authority
                         | Blockchain: Private

Supply Chain Tracking    | Supplier to Manufacturer Transaction ID, 
                         | Material Movement Logs
                         | Blockchain: Private

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
2. Artisan (Karigar) Entry – The Most Crucial Addition
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Ensures artisans receive fair wages, maintain authenticity, and allow buyers to verify craftsmanship.

Entry Type               | Data Fields
------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------
Artisan Registration     | Artisan ID, Name, Address, Aadhaar No, Govt. ID, 
                         | Specialization, Years of Experience, Contact
                         | Blockchain: Private

Workshop Association     | Artisan ID, Workshop ID, Craft Type
                         | Blockchain: Private

Skill Certification      | Certificate ID, Issuing Authority
                         | Blockchain: Private

Work Order Assignment    | Order ID, Artisan ID, Product Type, Assigned Date, 
                         | Expected Completion
                         | Blockchain: Private

Work Progress Tracking   | Work Logs (Spinning, Weaving, etc.), Timestamps, 
                         | Time Spent
                         | Blockchain: Private

Product Completion & QC  | Final Product ID, Artisan ID, Quality Control Report
                         | Blockchain: Private

Artisan Earnings         | Payment ID, Artisan ID, Work Completed, Wage Amount,
                         | Payment Status
                         | Blockchain: Private

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
3. Manufacturing Units & Workshops (Karigar Karkhanay)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Tracks conversion of raw materials into finished handicrafts.

Entry Type               | Data Fields
------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------
Workshop Registration    | Workshop ID, Owner Name, Location, Craft Type, No. of Artisans
                         | Blockchain: Private

Raw Material Intake      | Raw Material Batch ID, Date Received, Source (Linked)
                         | Blockchain: Private

Production Workflow      | Production ID, Assigned Artisans, Production Stages
                         | Blockchain: Private

Final Product Registration| Unique Product ID, Artisan Names, GI Cert.
                         | Blockchain: Private

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
4. Distributor Entry (Product Movement)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Tracks product movement to market.

Entry Type               | Data Fields
------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------
Distributor Registration | Distributor ID, Business Name, License, Location
                         | Blockchain: Private

Product Shipment Details | Product ID, Distributor ID, Date Dispatched, Destination
                         | Blockchain: Private

Warehouse Storage Logs   | Storage Facility ID, Conditions (Humidity, Temp)
                         | Blockchain: Private

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
5. Wholesaler Entry (Bulk Distribution)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Entry Type               | Data Fields
------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------
Wholesaler Registration  | Wholesaler ID, Business Name, License, Location
                         | Blockchain: Private

Bulk Purchase Orders     | Order ID, Distributor ID, Product Type, Quantity, Price
                         | Blockchain: Private

Inventory Management     | Product Stock ID, Quantity Available, Date of Stock Entry
                         | Blockchain: Private

Sales to Retailers       | Retailer ID, Date of Sale, Quantity, Payment Status
                         | Blockchain: Private

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
6. Retailer Entry (Selling to End Buyers)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Entry Type               | Data Fields
------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------
Retailer Registration    | Retailer ID, Business Name, Location, Online/Offline
                         | Blockchain: Private

Product Purchase         | Invoice ID, Wholesaler ID, Product Details
                         | Blockchain: Private

Stock Availability       | Product ID, Quantity, Pricing
                         | Blockchain: Private

Point-of-Sale Transaction| Buyer ID, Payment Method, Transaction Hash
                         | Blockchain: Private


-----------------------------------------------------------------------
7. Buyer Entry (Final Purchase & Ownership Transfer)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Entry Type               | Data Fields
------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------
Buyer Profile (Optional) | Buyer ID, Name, Location, Payment Method
                         | Blockchain: Private

Purchase Transaction     | Order ID, Retailer ID, Payment Confirmation
                         | Blockchain: Private

Ownership Registration   | Buyer Wallet Address, Product NFT ID, Transfer Date
                         | Blockchain: Private

Delivery Confirmation    | Buyer Acknowledgment, Time of Receipt
                         | Blockchain: Private

Reviews & Ratings        | Buyer ID, Product ID, Review, Star Rating
                         | Blockchain: Private

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
8. Post-Sale Services & Resale Market
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Entry Type               | Data Fields
------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------
Warranty Registration    | Buyer ID, Product ID, Warranty Period
                         | Blockchain: Private

Repair Logs              | Repair ID, Artisan IDs, Fix Type, Cost
                         | Blockchain: Private

Resale Transfer          | New Buyer ID, Transfer Date, Price
                         | Blockchain: Public

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
9. Sustainability & Ethical Trade Compliance
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Entry Type               | Data Fields
------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------
Sustainability Score     | CO2 Emissions, Water Usage
                         | Blockchain: Private

Fair Trade Compliance    | Artisan Wages, Working Conditions Report
                         | Blockchain: Private

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
